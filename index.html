About Me!

Hello and welcome to my portfolio website! My (legal) name is Muraz Maudarbux, but I mostly go by Mo. This site was initally created as part of my final year 'Front End Web Development' assignment, and I decided that this would be a great opportunity to create a great portfolio website, to showcase all my work, interests and creative style.

I'm currently a final year student studying at Computer Science at De Montfort University. This website will be used as an assignment for one of my final year modules, but I hope to continue using this site in the future, and develop and change it as I Design, Develop and get more experience in the industry. I also hope to update the site as industry standards change to keep up with the world of web Design.

The menu above will take you to the other pages of this one page site, that changes the content with JS. You'll be able to view my work as well as my CV and links to my social media such as Facebook, Twitter, Linked In, and even a link to my GitHub repositories! (Hint these can be found by clicking the logo, top right ;P ).

I hope you enjoy the site, and if you have any queries (Or job offers) feel free to contact me through the contact form!
